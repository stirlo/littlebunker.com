Nature Climate Change, Published online: 19 June 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-026-02665-2The economic impact of climate change has been widely acknowledged, yet current assessments remain fragmented and uncertain. Researchers develop a comprehensive channel-specific framework, apply it to the UK and show that the potential welfare loss could be much larger than previous estimates.
