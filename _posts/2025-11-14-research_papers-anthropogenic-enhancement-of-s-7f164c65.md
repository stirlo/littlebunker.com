Nature Climate Change, Published online: 14 November 2025; doi:10.1038/s41558-025-02458-zHow the conditions in soil layers below the surface change is not well understood. Here the authors assess changes in subsurface soil moisture, finding that these droughts also become more persistent and intense than surface droughts.
