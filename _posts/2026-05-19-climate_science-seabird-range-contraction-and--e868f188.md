Nature Climate Change, Published online: 19 May 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-026-02655-4The authors reconstruct historical seabird dispersal routes, showing that birds responded to temperature shifts by changes in range size rather than body mass. These trends are projected to persist, with higher rates of warming causing greater range contractions and longer dispersal distances.
