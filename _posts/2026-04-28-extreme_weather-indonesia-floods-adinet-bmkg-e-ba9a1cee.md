Country: Indonesia Source: European Commission's Directorate-General for European Civil Protection and Humanitarian Aid Operations In the past few days, Sarolangun Regency (Jambi Province, Sumatra Island) has experienced heavy rainfall, leading to flooding and resulting damage. According to the ASEAN Disaster Information Network (ADINet), almost 6 800 people have been affected, five houses have been destroyed, and four others have been damaged. Over the next three days, light rain is expected across most parts of the affected province.
