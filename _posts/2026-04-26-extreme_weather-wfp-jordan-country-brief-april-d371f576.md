Country: Jordan Source: World Food Programme Please refer to the attached file. KEY HIGHLIGHTS In March, WFP continued to provide reduced monthly food assistance of JOD 15 (USD 21) per person to 220,000 refugees in camps and host communities, covering only basic needs. From 1 April 2026, WFP food assistance was discontinued for an estimated 135,000 refugees residing in host communities due to lack of funding.. Activities supporting refugee self-reliance have begun in both Azraq and Zaatari camps, with approximately 160 participants receiving technical vocational training in various vocational tracks including IT, carpentry, welding, tailoring and others.
