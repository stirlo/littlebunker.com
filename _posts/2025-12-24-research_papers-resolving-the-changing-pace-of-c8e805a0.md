Nature Climate Change, Published online: 24 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s41558-025-02512-wWhether rivers are speeding up or slowing down in a warming Arctic is unclear, but has implications for carbon cycling and infrastructure. This study finds divergent behaviour in migration rates for rivers in discontinuous versus continuous permafrost, driven by changes in permafrost thaw and river ice.
