Nature Climate Change, Published online: 22 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s41558-025-02497-6Green subsidies (carrots) are now becoming a more politically acceptable climate policy option compared with corrective regulations (sticks). However, researcher show that carrots without quick and appropriate sticks will not be sufficient to reach the deep decarbonization goal in the long run.
