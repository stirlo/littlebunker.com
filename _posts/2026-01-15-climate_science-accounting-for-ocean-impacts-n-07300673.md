Nature Climate Change, Published online: 15 January 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-025-02533-5Oceans provide essential ecosystem services to human society, yet the climate impacts on blue capital have long been ignored. Incorporating the latest works on ocean science and economics, researchers show that accounting for the potential damage would almost double the social cost of carbon estimation.
