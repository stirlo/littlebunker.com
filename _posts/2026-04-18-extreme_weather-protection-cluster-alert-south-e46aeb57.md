Country: Sudan Source: Protection Cluster Please refer to the attached file. The Protection Cluster in Sudan is issuing a protection alert to highlight escalating risks facing civilians in Dilling locality, South Kordofan, amid intensified conflict, urban warfare, and growing isolation since early 2026. Ongoing insecurity, restricted movement, and disrupted supply routes have left over 50,000 residents and newly displaced people with limited access to essential services and humanitarian assistance, while exposure to violence and other protection risks continues to rise.Urgent, coordinated action is needed to ensure the protection of civilians, restore humanitarian access, and prevent further deterioration, particularly as the approaching rainy season is likely to deepen isolation and vulnerability.
