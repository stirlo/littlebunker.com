Nature Climate Change, Published online: 14 January 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-025-02510-yTransport networks in coastal zones are critical for human activities and are faced with increasing flooding risk. Using a detailed risk analysis in Europe, the authors show that the affected networks and expected annual damage will increase considerably with global warming.
