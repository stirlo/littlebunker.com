Nature Climate Change, Published online: 27 March 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-026-02606-zFaçade-integrated photovoltaics (FIPV) present a promising yet early-stage solution for mitigating building emissions. Combining global building datasets, climate projections and façade-scale simulations, researchers estimate that FIPV could deliver substantial economic and climate benefits.
