Country: Sudan Sources: CCCM Cluster, Protection Cluster, UN High Commissioner for Refugees Please refer to the attached file. No formal site management or coordination structure in place. Access roads exist, but the site layout is inadequate and unsafe. The site is flood-prone (near Roseires Dam), with exposure to scorpions, drowning, and waterborne diseases, The planned relocation of IDPs from Al Karama 3 before the rainy season remains under discussion among community leaders, authorities, and humanitarian partners. One proposed site was rejected by IDPs due to concerns over distance from services and land suitability. A second option is still being assessed, with water availability the main outstanding issue. Continued uncertainty is delaying decisions and discouraging investment in more durable shelter and site improvements at the current location, increasing exposure to rainy season risks.
