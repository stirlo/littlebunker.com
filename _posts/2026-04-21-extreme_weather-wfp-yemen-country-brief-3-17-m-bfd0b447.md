Country: Yemen Source: World Food Programme Please refer to the attached file. Continued regional tensions are heightening food security risks in Yemen while causing supply chain disruptions that are jeopardizing WFP's ability to stabilize food access. All United Nations Humanitarian Air Service (UNHAS) flights to and from Yemen have been suspended until further notice due to a lack of the necessary clearances. WFP continues to call for the release of its 38 staff members detained by the Sana'a-based authorities. All United Nations activities in northern Yemen remain suspended.
