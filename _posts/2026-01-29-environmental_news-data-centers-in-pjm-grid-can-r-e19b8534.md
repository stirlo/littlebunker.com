Regional grid operators said the federal order is a fail safe. Environmentals in Virginia worry about increased pollution regulators and residents may not be fully aware of.By Charles PaullinRICHMOND, Va.—The U.S. Department of Energy used the Federal Power Act on Monday to allow data centers within the regional grid operated by PJM Interconnection, including those in Virginia, to use generators at PJM’s discretion through Jan. 31. The allowance comes amid PJM’s record forecasts and demands for electricity and heat during freezing temps.
