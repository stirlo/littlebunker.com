Nature Climate Change, Published online: 03 June 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-026-02660-7How hailstorms change with warming is not well understood. Here the authors use global projections with different hail proxies to show that hail-prone conditions shift polewards under warming, also shifting crop risk related to hail hazards.
