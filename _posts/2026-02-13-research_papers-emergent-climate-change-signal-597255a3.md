Nature Climate Change, Published online: 13 February 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-026-02561-9The authors model the emergence of climate-driven changes in Antarctic sea ice, phytoplankton, krill, fish and penguins. They show earlier emergence for higher trophic levels, as well as highly seasonal and regional responses.
