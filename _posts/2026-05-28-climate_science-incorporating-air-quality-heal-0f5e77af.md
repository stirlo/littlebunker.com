Nature Climate Change, Published online: 28 May 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-026-02653-6Climate change worsens air pollution, posing major health risks, yet current social cost of carbon (SCC) models exclude these damages. This Review outlines a framework for including air quality impacts in the SCC and reviews existing evidence to inform near-term modelling efforts.
