Nature Climate Change, Published online: 03 June 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-026-02652-7The Southern Ocean is important for anthropogenic heat uptake, and this regional analysis shows an area with enhanced warming in the high-latitude Indian sector. Model analyses indicate that mesoscale eddies drive upward heat transport, linked to a strengthening of the Antarctic Circumpolar Current.
