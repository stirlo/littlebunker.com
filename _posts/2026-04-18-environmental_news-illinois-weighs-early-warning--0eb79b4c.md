What makes Illinois' bill distinct is the parks provision within the spray area, as studies point to particle drift and widespread injury across non-target public and private lands.By Gabriel Matias CastilhoA bill in the Illinois General Assembly would require certified pesticide users—anyone licensed by the Illinois Department of Agriculture to use Restricted Use pesticides, such as paraquat or fumigant insecticides—to give written or emailed notice at least 24 hours before application at any school, child care facility or park located within 1,500 feet of application that opted to receive them.
