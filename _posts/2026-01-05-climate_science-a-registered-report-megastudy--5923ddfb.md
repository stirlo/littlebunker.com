Nature Climate Change, Published online: 05 January 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-025-02536-2How to effectively communicate climate change to the public has long been studied and debated. Through a registered report megastudy, researchers tested the ten most-cited climate change messaging strategies published, finding that many had significant, but small, effects on climate change attitudes.
