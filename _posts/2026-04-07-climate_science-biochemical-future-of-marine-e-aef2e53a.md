Nature Climate Change, Published online: 31 March 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-026-02590-4Warming oceans will alter not only how much phytoplankton grow, but what they are made of and how they function within marine food webs. Now a mechanistic model shows how environmental change reshapes cellular composition, offering a path towards more physiologically grounded marine ecosystem projections.
