Nature Climate Change, Published online: 21 November 2025; doi:10.1038/s41558-025-02498-5The authors consider studies reporting species range shifts and demonstrate a geometric bias in sampling along latitudinal, rather than longitudinal, gradients. This bias may favour the corroboration of shift expectations with warming and mask other patterns and drivers of species movements.
