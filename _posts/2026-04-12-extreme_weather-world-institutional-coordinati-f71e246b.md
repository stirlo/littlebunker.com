Institutional coordination for drought resilience: join IDMP and FAO's virtual exchange on 14 April As drought events grow more frequent and severe across the globe, the ability of institutions to work together has never been more critical. The Integrated Drought Management Programme (IDMP) and FAO's Land and Water Division (NSL) are hosting a virtual exchange on Tuesday, 14 April 2026, bringing together experts and country representatives to explore what makes institutional coordination for drought resilience work in practice.
