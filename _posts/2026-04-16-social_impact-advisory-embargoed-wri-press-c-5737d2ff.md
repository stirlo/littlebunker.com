Registration is for members of the media only. WASHINGTON D.C. (April 16, 2026) – Join World Resources Institute (WRI) and the University of Maryland's GLAD Lab (UMD) on April 23, 2026, for an embargoed press call previewing how much global tree cover was lost in 2025, along with expert analysis of the state of the world's forests.The call will take place at 8AM EDT (2PM CEST / 7PM WIB) and will feature global forest experts from WRI and UMD. To attend, please register here.Speakers will present new forest loss data from UMD's GLAD Lab, highlighting 2025 hotspots, key drivers of loss, and impacts on climate, biodiversity and livelihoods. They will also outline solutions to strengthen forest protection.Please note: The 2025 Tree Cover Loss data and analysis are strictly embargoed until April 29, 2026 at 12:01 AM EDT / 6:01 AM CEST / 11:01 AM WIB. By registering for this press call, you agree to respect this embargo.The call will be held in English, with live interpretation into French, Spanish, Portuguese and Bahasa Indonesian.The UMD GLAD Lab's 2025 tree cover loss data will be released on Global Forest Watch and Global Nature Watch on April 29, with in-depth analysis available on the Global Forest Review.WHATEmbargoed press call to preview 2025 Global Tree Cover Loss DataWHENThursday, April 23, 2026, at 8:00AM (2:00PM CEST / 7:00PM WIB)WHOSpeakers: Elizabeth Goldman, Co-Director, Global Forest Watch, WRISarah Carter, Research Associate, Global Forest Watch, WRIMatthew Hansen, Professor, University of Maryland; Director, Global Land Analysis and Discovery (GLAD) LabRespondents: Arief Wijaya, Managing Director, WRI IndonesiaJoaquin Carrizosa, Senior Advisor, WRI ColombiaMirela Sandrini, Executive Director, WRI BrasilPeter Potapov, Researcher, Food, Land and Water, WRI; Adjunct Professor, Department of Geographical Sciences, University of MarylandRod Taylor, Global Director, Forests, WRIStasiek Czaplicki Cabezas, Bolivian researcher and data journalist for Revista NómadasTeodyl Nkuintchua, Congo Basin Strategy and Engagement Leader, WRI AfricaModerator: Alison Cinnamond, Global Director, Strategic Communications, WRIWHERETo RSVP, please register here.
