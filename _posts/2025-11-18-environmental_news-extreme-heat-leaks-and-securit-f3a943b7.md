The United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change raised multiple concerns about the makeshift venue in Belém.By Bob BerwynBELÉM, Brazil—The United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change asked the host government to address complaints about stifling indoor conditions, leaky roofs and non-working restrooms at COP30. It also raised concerns about security at the climate talks after demonstrators entered the negotiation area without the required credentials.
