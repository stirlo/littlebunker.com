Nature Climate Change, Published online: 01 May 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-026-02628-7Addressing non-CO2 greenhouse gases alongside CO2 is essential for climate mitigation, but distributional effects remain a major concern. Now a study shows that when climate policy extends beyond CO2, the resulting costs are unevenly distributed across households worldwide.
