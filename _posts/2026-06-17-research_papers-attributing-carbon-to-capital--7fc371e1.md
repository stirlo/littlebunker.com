Nature Climate Change, Published online: 17 June 2026; doi:10.1038/s41558-026-02654-5Greenhouse gas emissions are typically attributed to where they are produced or consumed. A recent analysis traces them to capital owners, revealing a stronger concentration and offering new perspectives on how they are distributed — and why it matters.
