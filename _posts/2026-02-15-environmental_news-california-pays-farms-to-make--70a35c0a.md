Last year, six farms were the first in the Tar Heel State to get funding from California's program offsetting its transportation emissions. Their permits to make biogas already have civil rights complaints against them for the pollution from the process.By Blanca BegertTURKEY, N.C.—The Align RNG biogas processing facility here is so small, you would miss it if you weren’t looking for it. Just four small silver mounds beside a massive 100-foot grain silo under which trucks drive day-in, day-out loading up with hog feed.
