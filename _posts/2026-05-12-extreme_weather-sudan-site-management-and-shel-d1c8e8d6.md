Country: Sudan Sources: CCCM Cluster, Shelter Cluster Please refer to the attached file. This plan sets out the joint Site Management and Shelter/NFI approach to anticipatory action, emergency response, and early recovery for the 2026 rainy and flood season in Sudan. It focuses on flood-prone displacement sites, collective centres, site-like settings, and high-risk host community locations hosting or directly affected by IDP displacement, where seasonal flooding is likely to drive secondary displacement, shelter loss, service disruption, and protection risks. The 2026 Sudan Preparedness and Response Plan emphasizes proactive measures rather than just reacting to emergencies. It calls for funding early actions and risk reduction in shelter and site safety ahead of peak rains. The plan aligns with the 2026 Humanitarian Needs Response Plan (HNRP), incorporating Disaster Risk Reduction activities within Site Management and Emergency Shelter/NFI. Additionally, it is intended to complement a joint advocacy note, reinforcing that disaster risk reduction and site safety during the rainy season are essential life-saving priorities, especially when funding is limited.
