Nature Climate Change, Published online: 24 December 2025; doi:10.1038/s41558-025-02525-5Climate change drives displacement and migration across the Americas, particularly exposing Latin American and Caribbean children to compounded health risks. We explore these health impacts, identify gaps in related US healthcare and health policy, and propose recommendations for how they can respond.
