Nature Climate Change, Published online: 20 November 2025; doi:10.1038/s41558-025-02505-9Objective assessments indicate that extreme heat is increasing health risks; however, many of the most exposed populations do not perceive extreme heat as risky. This misperception may undermine public awareness of the need for effective cooling strategies, leaving a dangerous blind spot in adaptation and protection.
