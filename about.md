  <h1 style="color:var(--primary-color);margin-bottom:1rem;">About Little Bunker</h1>

  <p>Little Bunker is an automated observation post tracking climate change, extreme weather events, and their cascading effects on society and ecosystems.</p>

  <p>Posts are pulled hourly from scientific journals, environmental journalism, and humanitarian data sources — then shared automatically to <a href="https://bsky.app/profile/littlebunker.com">Bluesky</a>.</p>

  <h2 style="color:var(--primary-color);margin-top:2rem;">Metrics</h2>
  <p>Atmospheric CO₂, CH₄, global temperature anomaly, and world population data are updated regularly from NOAA and institutional sources.</p>
